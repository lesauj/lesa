WELCOME TO LeSA UJ

Lesotho Students Association University of Johnnesburg (LeSA UJ) is a society of Basotho students enrolled at the University of Johanneburg. LeSA UJ is part of United Lesotho Students Association which is an umbrella of all Basotho Students Associations in tertiary schools in Lesotho and abroad.

Our Mission

As a student body operating under the University of Johannesburg and ULSA, there are a lot of regulations to follow, but we still have a mission solely meant to better the lives of Basotho students at UJ.

Membership

To Keep the society running well and healthy, we ask our beloved Basotho Students to make a contribution to the society in the form of yearly affliation of 100.00LSL

Merchandise

As LeSA UJ we want to be unqiue and special out there in the world of COVID-19 so we sell our products at a low price so that our society can be represented properly.
